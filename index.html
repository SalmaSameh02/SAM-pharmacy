Business Overview

SAM pharmacy is a pharmacy in Giza. It provides a wide range of branded and generic medicines, medical devices, personal care products and other basic everyday needs. It aims to be a pharmacy that offers supreme service and easy access to the local residents. In order to meet its customers needs, SAM pharmacy is open 24/7.

SAM pharmacy is the brainchild of blah.. blahh, a pharmacist, working at a local drug store in Egypt. Through her 5 years of experience in the medical field, the Founder saw the need for an accessible pharmacy that stocks all major medicines and medical devices and offers them at an affordable rate.
